About WiFi QR Codes

WiFi QR codes allow guests to connect to your network without manually entering the password. When scanned with a smartphone camera, the device automatically connects to the WiFi network.

How This Generator Works

Our WiFi QR code generator creates customized QR codes based on your network settings:

  • Network Name (SSID): Your WiFi network's name
  • Encryption Type: Security protocol used by your network
  • Password: Network password (not stored or saved)
  • Network Visibility: Whether your network is broadcasted or hidden

Best Practices

Follow these tips for effective WiFi QR code usage:

  • Place QR codes in visible locations for guests
  • Use a strong password with a mix of characters
  • Choose WPA/WPA2 encryption for best security
  • Print QR codes on durable material for long-term use
  • Test your QR code before sharing it with others

Security Considerations

When creating WiFi QR codes:

  • QR codes contain your network password in plain text
  • Only share with trusted individuals
  • Consider creating a guest network instead of sharing your main network
  • Regularly update your WiFi password for security
  • Remove QR codes when no longer needed

How to Use Your WiFi QR Code

  1. Generate and download your QR code
  2. Print it or display it on a device
  3. Guests scan the code with their phone camera
  4. They tap the notification to connect automatically
