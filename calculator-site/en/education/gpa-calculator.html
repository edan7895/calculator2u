How GPA Calculator Works

This tool calculates your GPA using the following formula:

GPA = Σ(Grade Points × Credit Hours) / Σ(Credit Hours)

  • Converts letter grades to numerical values based on the selected grading scale
  • Multiplies each grade by the corresponding credit hours
  • Sums all quality points and divides by total credit hours

Supported Grading Scales

  • 4.0 Scale: A=4.0, A-=3.7, B+=3.3, B=3.0, B-=2.7, C+=2.3, C=2.0, C-=1.7, D+=1.3, D=1.0, F=0.0
  • 5.0 Scale: A=5.0, B=4.0, C=3.0, D=2.0, F=0.0
  • Custom Scale: Assign your own grade point values
  • Percentage Conversion: 93-100=A, 85-92=B+, 77-84=B, 69-76=B-, 63-69=C+, 55-62=C, 45-54=D, 0-44=F
