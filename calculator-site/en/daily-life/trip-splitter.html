How Trip Splitter Works

This calculator helps you fairly split travel expenses among participants. It tracks:

  • Who paid for each expense
  • The amount of each expense
  • The category of each expense
  • Who owes whom and how much
  • Custom splits: Choose specific participants for each expense

Common Trip Expenses

Here are some common expenses that can be split:

  • Accommodation: Hotel, Airbnb, or other lodging costs
  • Transportation: Flights, car rentals, gas, tolls, public transport
  • Food: Meals, groceries, snacks
  • Entertainment: Activities, attractions, events
  • Other: Souvenirs, tips, miscellaneous costs

Trip Planning Tips

Consider these tips to manage trip expenses effectively:

  • Budget in advance to avoid overspending
  • Agree on how expenses will be split before the trip
  • Use custom splits for expenses that don't include everyone
  • Keep all receipts and payment records
  • Settle accounts promptly after the trip
  • Use this calculator to make the process fair and transparent
