Typing Speed Standards

Typing speeds are generally categorized as:

  • Beginner: 20-30 WPM
  • Intermediate: 30-45 WPM
  • Advanced: 45-65 WPM
  • Professional: 65-90 WPM
  • Expert: 90+ WPM

With regular practice, most people can reach 60+ WPM within a few months.
