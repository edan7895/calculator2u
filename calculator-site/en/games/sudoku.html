How to Play:

Fill the grid so that every row, every column, and every 3x3 box contains the digits 1 to 9 without repeating. Click a cell to select it, then choose a number to place in the cell.

About Sudoku Game

Sudoku is a classic logic-based puzzle that challenges your problem-solving skills. Our implementation provides various difficulty levels and features to enhance your experience.

Game Features

  • Multiple difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard)
  • Auto-generated puzzles with unique solutions
  • Hint system to help when stuck
  • Error highlighting for incorrect moves
  • Timer and score tracking
  • Responsive design for all devices

Benefits of Playing Sudoku

Regularly playing Sudoku can:

  • Improve logical thinking and concentration
  • Enhance memory and cognitive abilities
  • Reduce stress and anxiety
  • Provide a sense of accomplishment
  • Help prevent cognitive decline

Scoring System

Your score is calculated based on:

  • Difficulty level (higher difficulty = higher base score)
  • Completion time (faster = higher score)
  • Number of hints used (fewer hints = higher score)
  • Errors made (fewer errors = higher score)
