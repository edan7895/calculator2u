Important Health Considerations

Body fat percentage is a better indicator of fitness and health than BMI alone. Maintaining a healthy body fat percentage can reduce your risk of chronic diseases.

Recommendations

To maintain or improve your body composition:

  • Engage in regular cardiovascular exercise
  • Incorporate strength training into your routine
  • Follow a balanced diet with adequate protein
  • Monitor your progress over time
  • Consider consulting with a fitness professional

Understanding Body Fat

Body fat percentage represents the total mass of fat divided by total body weight. It is a more accurate measure of health than BMI alone. Healthy body fat ranges vary by age and gender.

Body Fat Categories

Body fat percentage can be categorized as:

  • Essential Fat: 2-5% for men, 10-13% for women
  • Athletes: 6-13% for men, 14-20% for women
  • Fitness: 14-17% for men, 21-24% for women
  • Acceptable: 18-24% for men, 25-31% for women
  • Obese: 25%+ for men, 32%+ for women

Body Fat Calculation Method

This calculator uses the U.S. Navy circumference method, which estimates body fat using gender, age, weight, height, and circumferences of neck, waist, and hip.
